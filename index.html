About Bishal

The Person
Behind the Lens

"I'm a photographer passionate about capturing real moments and genuine emotions in a simple and timeless way."

Based in the Bay Area, I specialize in creating photographs that feel authentic and alive. Whether it's the quiet glance between two people in love, the unscripted laughter of a family, or the grandeur of a wedding day — I'm here to preserve those fleeting moments forever.

Proudly serving San Jose, San Francisco, Oakland, Palo Alto, Silicon Valley, and all of the Bay Area.
